## 1. Introduction
*   **Purpose**: Why this software?
*   **Scope**: What it does (and doesn't do).
*   **Definitions**: Glossary.

## 2. Overall Description
*   **Product Perspective**: System interfaces, constraints.
*   **Product Functions**: Summary of capability.
*   **User Characteristics**: Who uses this?
*   **Constraints**: Regulatory, hardware, etc.

## 3. Specific Requirements
*   **Functional Requirements**: "The system shall..."
*   **External Interface Requirements**: UI, Hardware, Software, Comm.
*   **Performance Requirements**: Latency, throughput.
*   **Design Constraints**: Standards compliance.
*   **Attributes**: Reliability, Availability, Security, Maintainability.
